About

I'm currently studying at Simon Fraser University in the School of Interactive Arts & Technology (BSc) in Surrey, B.C., with concentrations in Extended Reality & Game Design and Web Design & Development.

I am game designer with a focus on gameplay & level design. I'm most interested in developing core and secondary mechanics, helping create engaging gameplay loops for players. I've built my experience through several team-based projects creating completed games as a mechanic designer, level designer, and project manager.

My skillset in programming (Java/Unity) and prototyping tools (Figma) has allowed me to develop, iterate, and test my design decisions in these projects, as I can communicate through both sketches and working prototypes. My skillset also allows me to closely collaborate with designers and programmers, helping bridge the gap between functionality and aesthetics for games.

Outside of game design, I am also interested in web development, having created websites using HTML, CSS, and PHP. I have been actively expanding my skillset in coding, as I am completing a Certificate in Computing Science at SFU.
