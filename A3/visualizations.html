About the Data

This dataset is a collection of video game sales data. It contains the following variables:

Name

The title of the video game

Type: Nominal

Platform

Which system the game was made for

Type: Nominal, Categorical

Year

The year the game was released

Type: Ordinal, Quantitative

Genre

The genre of the video game

Type: Nominal, Categorical

Publisher

The publisher of the video game

Type: Nominal, Categorical

NA_Sales

Sales in North America (in millions of units)

Type: Quantitative

EU_Sales

Sales in Europe (in millions of units)

Type: Quantitative

JP_Sales

Sales in Japan (in millions of units)

Type: Quantitative

Other_Sales

Sales in other regions (in millions of units)

Type: Quantitative

Global_Sales

Total global sales (in millions of units)

Type: Quantitative
