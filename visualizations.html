A3: Vega-Lite Intro

Visualization 1: Create a visualization to explore global sales by genre and platform.

This visualization uses a bar chart to show the global sales of games for different platforms. Additionally, the distribution of genres is shown using colour. Hovering over each section in the bars shows how many sales belong to that platform and genre combination.

Visualization 2: Visualize sales trends over time, focusing on platforms and genres.

This visualization uses a line chart to show how global sales of games have changed overtime. To highlight the prominent platforms and genres throughout the years, hovering over each point shows which platform and genre was the most popular for that year in terms of game sales.

Visualization 3: Compare the sales between different regions (NA, EU, JP, and Others) by platform.

This visualization uses a line chart to show how game sales vary by platform and region. Each region is represented through its own coloured line, and each platform is represented on the x-axis. Compare the performance of a platform in different regions by looking at a vertical stack of dots. Hovering over the dots shows more information, like the exact number of sales.

Visualization 4: Compare the global sales between the Wii and WiiU.

This visualization uses a point chart to show the performance of the Wii vs. WiiU over time. Each platform is distinguished through colour, showing how the sales trended with the introduction of a new replacement console.
